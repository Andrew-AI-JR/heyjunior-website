## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

### What NOT to Do:

1. **Don't Lie About Numbers**
   - If you don't have 1,200+ users yet, adjust the number
   - Better to start with "250+ beta users" and update as you grow

2. **Don't Overuse Urgency**
   - Fake countdown timers destroy trust
   - Only use scarcity if it's real

3. **Don't Ignore Mobile**
   - 40-60% of traffic is mobile
   - All changes are mobile-responsive

4. **Don't Skip the Lead Magnet**
   - This captures 10-15% of visitors who won't buy immediately
   - Build email list for nurture campaigns

5. **Don't Forget to Test**
   - Not every change works for every audience
   - A/B test major changes
