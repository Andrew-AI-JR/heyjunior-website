# How to Become a Junior Partner

Welcome! Here's everything you need to get set up as a Junior partner and start earning commissions.

---

## What You Get

- **20% commission** on every subscription payment from people who sign up using your link
- Commission lasts **for the lifetime** of each subscription you refer (as long as they stay subscribed)
- Payouts handled through **Stripe** or **Wise**, depending on your country — directly to your bank account
- **International support** — partners in 50+ countries can participate
- A **personal dashboard** where you can track your earnings and signups

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Payout Account

After approval, log in to your Junior account and go to your partner dashboard:

**https://heyjunior.ai/reseller-dashboard.html**

You'll be asked to **select your country**. Based on your country, you'll be set up with the right payout method:

### Stripe Connect (US, UK, Europe, Canada, Switzerland)
Click **"Connect with Stripe"** and follow the steps to connect your bank account. Stripe handles everything securely — we never see your bank details. Commissions are deposited per transaction.

### Wise (Brazil, Mexico, Australia, Japan, and 40+ other countries)
Enter your **payout email address**. Commissions are sent via Wise every Monday, with a **$20 minimum threshold**. Wise transfer fees are deducted from your commission. If your accrued amount is below $20, it rolls over to the next week.

If you get interrupted during setup, just come back to the dashboard and pick up where you left off.

---

## Step 3: Share Your Personal Link

Once your Stripe account is connected, your dashboard will show your **personal referral link**. It looks like this:

```
https://heyjunior.ai/?ref=YOURCODE
```

(Your actual code will be shown on your dashboard -- just click "Copy" to grab it.)

Share this link anywhere:
- Social media posts
- Direct messages
- Email newsletters
- Blog posts
- YouTube video descriptions
- Anywhere you talk to job seekers

When someone clicks your link and signs up for Junior, they're automatically connected to your account. You earn 20% of every payment they make.

---

## Step 4: Track Your Earnings

Visit your dashboard anytime to see:

- **Total earnings** (all-time)
- **This month's earnings**
- **Number of signups** from your link
- **Which signups are active subscribers**

To manage your payout settings or see transfer history, click **"Open Stripe Express"** on your dashboard.

---

## How It Works Behind the Scenes

1. Someone clicks your link (e.g., `heyjunior.ai/?ref=YOURCODE`)
2. Your referral code is saved in their browser for **30 days**
3. When they create an account and subscribe, your code is attached to their account
4. Every time they pay their subscription, **20% goes to you** automatically via Stripe
5. This continues for as long as they remain a subscriber

---

## Important Notes

- Your referral link works for **30 days** after someone clicks it. If they sign up within that window, you get credit.
- If someone already clicked a different partner's link, the first link takes priority.
- Commissions are calculated on the net amount (after Stripe processing fees and any refunds).
- You can see your dashboard and earnings at any time by logging in at **https://heyjunior.ai/reseller-dashboard.html**.
