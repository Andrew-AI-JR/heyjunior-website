Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

❓ Will LinkedIn ban my account?

Junior is architecturally designed to minimize detection risk. Unlike server-based tools that LinkedIn can easily fingerprint, Junior runs entirely on your own computer — your browser, your IP, your natural environment. We also use human-behavioral modeling (variable delays, natural scroll patterns, reading pauses), contextually relevant, unique comments (never templated), and conservative daily rate limits that stay within normal activity ranges. The tool is designed to keep your engagement natural and authentic, and we continuously update it as LinkedIn evolves. That said, any third-party tool carries inherent platform risk — please review our Terms of Service for full details.

🤔 Is this ethical?

Yes. Junior helps you participate in the kind of conversations you would engage in manually if you had the time. It generates genuine, contextual engagement — not spam. Many professionals use similar tools to manage their LinkedIn presence efficiently.

💡 How is this different from LinkedIn Premium?

LinkedIn Premium shows you who viewed your profile and gives you InMail credits. Junior actively engages WITH hiring managers FOR you - posting thoughtful comments, building relationships, and getting you noticed. It's like having a personal networking assistant working 24/7.

📊 What if I don't get results?

100% money-back guarantee within 30 days. No questions asked. We're confident you'll see engagement within the first 2 weeks, but if Junior isn't working for you, just email us for a full refund.

⚙️ Is it difficult to set up?

Not at all. Download the app, connect your LinkedIn (secure browser login), set your preferences, and you're done. Takes less than 5 minutes. No technical skills required. On first run, Junior opens a Chrome window so you can log in to LinkedIn once — after that, it remembers your session. See the full first-launch guide.

🔒 Is my LinkedIn account safe?

Yes. You log in directly on LinkedIn's own page via a secure browser window — Junior never sees or stores your password. Your data is encrypted, and the tool runs locally on your computer — not on our servers. You're always in control.

💬 Do the comments sound natural?

Yes! Our AI is trained on thousands of successful LinkedIn interactions. Comments are personalized to each post, relevant to the content, and sound genuinely human. The result reads like a thoughtful professional engaging authentically — not like a bot.

📅 Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts or commitments. Cancel with one click from your account dashboard. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

🎯 How many comments does it make per day?

You can customize this, but we recommend 40-60 interactions per day to stay natural. That's 1,200-1,800 per month - all personalized, all targeted at hiring managers in your industry.

💰 What's the difference between Standard and Pro?

Standard gives you up to 50 engagements per day, perfect for most job seekers. Pro increases that to 80 engagements per day, plus priority support and early access to new features. Choose Pro if you're in an active, intensive job search or want maximum LinkedIn visibility.

🖥️ What platforms does it work on?

Windows 10/11 and macOS (both Intel and Apple Silicon M1/M2). The desktop app runs in the background and works seamlessly while you do other things.

📈 How quickly will I see results?

Most users report their first hiring manager response within 3-7 days. By week 2, you should have multiple conversations going. Remember: networking is a numbers game, and Junior plays it better than manual effort ever could.
