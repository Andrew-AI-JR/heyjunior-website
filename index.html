Your reputation stays in your hands

Junior is built as a local assistant, not a cloud bot. Here is how it protects your brand.

Runs on your machine

Your own browser, your own IP, your own session. No shared servers or cross-account fingerprinting.

Contextual, not templated

Every comment is drafted from the post content and your profile context. No recycled templates.

Paced like a human

Variable timing, natural scroll patterns, and conservative daily limits keep activity within normal ranges.

Platform risk disclosed

Any third-party engagement tool carries inherent platform risk. We design for minimal detection surface, but we do not promise zero risk.

How Junior finds the right conversations

Not every LinkedIn post is worth your time. Junior filters for the ones that matter.

1
Scans your LinkedIn feed

Junior monitors posts from your network and extended reach based on your configured interests.

2
Filters for high-signal activity

Posts are ranked by relevance, author role, engagement momentum, and alignment with your goals.

3
Drafts a contextual comment

Each comment is generated from the post content and your profile so it reads like something you would actually say.

4
Engages and tracks replies

Comments are posted at human-like intervals. You can review engagement quality and replies in your analytics.
