My Story

I want to share how I found success in this terrible job market

After being laid off twice since 2022 and having to sell my house, I know firsthand how brutal this job market is. I'm not alone - it's really difficult out there for so many people.

Success in job hunting isn't about volume resume blasts. It's about conversations with real people who can connect you to real opportunities.

The problem I faced was simple: I couldn't get enough conversations with the right people. In six weeks, I had just one interview. That interview came from a single comment I left on a post about a company hiring. The person replied the same day, we spoke the next day, and a week later I was talking to the hiring manager.

Job seeking shouldn't be about hoping for a conversation - it should start with one.

That's why I built Junior. It's an automated inbound personal marketing engine that helped me turn things around. It worked so well that I decided to start a company so you can have your own Junior.

If you're struggling to start conversations with the right people, I encourage you to sign up for our Beta test.

Have the right conversation, at the right time. Unlock new opportunities that can change your life. It changed mine.

Warm regards,

Andrew Malinow, PhD
